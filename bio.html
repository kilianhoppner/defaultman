BIOGRAPHY

Hello! My name is Kilian (he/him) and I'm a graphic designer and artist based in London. In my visual exploration, I'm mostly silly but can sometimes be serious, reminding us of our collective responsibility. We're in this together, remember? Recently, my work looks at the department of masculinity, imagining futures where men know how to be men. Grayson Perry's writing has been especially influential here, guiding my research. Stepping back, I realise my life and creative pursuits basically merge into one. Like a Venn diagram with only overlap. When I do take time off, I spend time with family and friends, snap film photos, go hiking and enjoy a good techno rave at FOLD. I'm also training for a triathlon (please God have mercy) (such a struggle, would not recommend).
